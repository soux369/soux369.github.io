Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

1. Introduction

"Mirai's Schedule" (the "App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the App.

2. Data Collection and Usage

2.1 User Data

Timetable data, assignments, and notes are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

2.2 AI Image Analysis

When using the "Scan Timetable" feature, images are temporarily sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. Images are discarded immediately after analysis and are not used for model training.

2.3 Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. Anonymous user IDs and purchase history (product ID, price, date) are processed to provide premium features. Payment information is handled exclusively by Apple or Google.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide and improve the App:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising. May collect advertising IDs (IDFA/AAID).
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered timetable scanning.

4. Data Deletion

All app data is stored locally. You can delete all your data by uninstalling the App from your device.

5. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us via the support form linked on our website.
